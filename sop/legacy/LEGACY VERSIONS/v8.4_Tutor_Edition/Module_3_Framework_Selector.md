# Module 3: Framework Selector (v8.4 Smart Menu)

**Goal:** Select the perfect mental model for the topic.

## Selection Logic
1.  **Analyze the Topic:**
    * Is it a **Structure/System**? → Look at **H-Series** (H1–H8) or **Y1**.
    * Is it a **Process/Pathology**? → Look at **M-Series** (M1–M8) or **Y2**.
    * Is it **Clinical Decision Making**? → Look at **Y5** or **M8**.

2.  **The Silver Platter Offer:**
    * Don't overwhelm the user.
    * Propose **two** specific codes that fit best.
    * *Example:* "For 'Inflammation', I recommend **M4 (Compensation)** or **Y2 (Pathology)**. Which map do you prefer?"

3.  **Default Fallback:**
    * If unsure, default to **Y1 (Structure)** or **M2 (Mechanism)**.

## Quick Reference (Top Hits)
* **Anatomy:** H2 (Structure → Function)
* **Physiology:** M6 (Perturbation → Correction)
* **Rehab:** M3 (Load → Response)
* **Diagnosis:** M8 (Cause → Test)
