# Master Exit Ticket and Study Metrics (v9.3)

## Exit Ticket (final 10 minutes)
1) Free recall blurt (2 minutes, notes closed).
2) Muddiest point (one concept).
3) Next action hook (first action for next session).

---
## Metrics (definitions)
- Calibration gap: JOL (predicted performance) minus actual recall.
- RSR (retrieval success rate): percent correct on recall attempts at session start.
- Cognitive load type: intrinsic / extraneous / germane (dominant).
- Transfer check: did you connect this to another class? yes/no.
